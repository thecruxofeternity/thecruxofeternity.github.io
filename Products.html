In a world shaped by a select few masters of the natural elements, the visions of a long-forgotten queen foretell a crossroads in the future of humanity. Oblivious to their own significance, four strangers will dictate the fate of the world, and all life balances on the knife edge of their choices.

One decisive night will gather them all together under the same roof, where the flickering fires of life will continue to burn... or be extinguished forever.
