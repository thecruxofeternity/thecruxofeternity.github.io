Author Bio

When he was fourteen, Lane Trompeter said he wanted to write, but writing doesn't feed you until it does. So until his words could fill his plate, he would teach and coach. He's lived that promise ever since, and it's time (finally) for his words to pull their weight.

Built for Montana but living in Florida, Lane survives the heat through the unfailing love of his wife, Ellen, and the unwavering belief that the world might some day be a better place if we take the time to mold children into human beings.
